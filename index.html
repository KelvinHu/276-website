Who are you?

I think most readers who end up on this site will inevitably ask themselves who I am; to put it simply, I don't really know who I am either. What I can tell you is that I am a studying for a computer science major at Simon Fraser University; I've been studying for longer than I'd like to admit, but I am well on my way to finishing my post-secondary educational goals. My education aside, I have an interest in cats, food, and, as the subject of this blog, all things computer science related; though not all posts may have a focus on the science part of computer science.
